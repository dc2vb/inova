Phase 1: Accelerated Discovery & Core Requirements (Weeks 1-3)

Objective: Rapidly define critical business objectives for a pilot AEP implementation, identify key stakeholders, and outline essential data sources within a compressed timeframe, with a strong focus on **CJA and CDP use cases**.

Key Activities:

  • 1.1 Stakeholder & Business Requirements Gathering: Conduct in-depth interviews with key stakeholders across Marketing, IT, Patient Experience, Clinical Operations, Data Science, and Legal/Compliance. Map current patient journeys and identify critical pain points and opportunities for personalization that **CJA can illuminate and CDP can activate**. Define core marketing intelligence use cases (e.g., patient acquisition, retention, service line growth, personalized health content delivery, appointment scheduling optimization) that directly leverage **unified patient profiles in CDP and journey analysis in CJA**.
  • *My Contribution:* Leverage my client engagement and strategic relationship management skills to ensure all voices are heard and business needs are accurately translated into technical requirements for **CJA and CDP**.
  • 1.2 Current State Assessment & Data Ecosystem Audit: Document existing data sources (CRM, EMR/EHR integrations, website analytics, marketing automation platforms, call center data, mobile app data, third-party data) that will feed into **AEP's Real-time Customer Profile (CDP)** and **Customer Journey Analytics**. Analyze current data quality, completeness, and accessibility, specifically identifying gaps for **comprehensive patient profiles and journey data**. Assess existing analytics tools and reporting capabilities to identify gaps **CJA and CDP** will fill.
  • 1.3 Data Governance, Privacy & Compliance Framework: Collaborate closely with Legal and Compliance teams to establish a robust data governance framework, ensuring strict adherence to HIPAA, patient privacy regulations, and Inova's internal policies for **data flowing into AEP's CDP and CJA**. Define data ownership, access controls, and data retention policies within the AEP context, with particular attention to **patient data within CDP**.
  • *My Contribution:* My experience with complex web ecosystems and government organizations has instilled a deep understanding of stringent compliance requirements, which is paramount in healthcare, especially concerning **patient data in CDP**.
  • 1.4 Success Metrics & ROI Definition: Establish clear, measurable Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for the AEP implementation, tied directly to Inova's strategic goals (e.g., increase in patient lifetime value, reduction in cost per acquisition, improvement in patient satisfaction scores, enhanced campaign ROI) that can be **tracked and reported in CJA**. Develop a framework for ongoing measurement and reporting of AEP's impact, focusing on **CJA's analytical capabilities**.

Deliverables:

  • Comprehensive Current State & Gap Analysis Report
  • Detailed Business Requirements & Prioritized Use Case Matrix (with **CJA/CDP focus**)
  • AEP Data Governance & Privacy Framework (initial draft), specifically for **CDP data**
  • Defined KPIs and ROI Measurement Plan (with **CJA reporting emphasis**)

Phase 2: Foundational Architecture & Data Model (Weeks 4-6)

Objective: Design the optimal AEP architecture, data model, and integration strategy tailored to Inova's unique healthcare data landscape, specifically for **CJA and CDP functionality**.

Key Activities:

  • 2.1 AEP Solution Architecture Design: Architect the end-to-end AEP solution, including data ingestion methods (streaming for real-time interactions for **CDP**, batch for historical data for **CJA**), data lake structure, and data flow orchestration. Define the role of Experience Platform Identity Service for robust patient identity resolution across disparate sources, crucial for **unified profiles in CDP and cross-channel journeys in CJA**.
  • 2.2 Experience Data Model (XDM) Design: Develop comprehensive XDM schemas for critical healthcare entities (e.g., patient profiles, clinical encounters, marketing interactions, financial transactions, service line engagements) that are optimized for **Real-time Customer Profile and Customer Journey Analytics**. Ensure XDM design supports **CJA's analytical capabilities** for granular journey analysis and **CDP's real-time profile unification** for segmentation and activation.
  • *My Contribution:* My background in optimizing complex data structures for SEO and digital performance will be applied to ensure the data model is both comprehensive and efficient for **CJA analytics and CDP activation**.
  • 2.3 Identity Resolution Strategy: Design a sophisticated identity resolution strategy to accurately unify patient identities across online and offline touchpoints, respecting privacy constraints, forming the backbone of **CDP's unified profile**. Define the hierarchy and rules for identity merging within AEP, directly impacting the accuracy of **CDP segments and CJA journey paths**.
  • 2.4 CJA Workspace & Data View Design: Plan out **CJA workspaces**, defining data views, metrics, dimensions, and segments to support specific patient journey analyses identified in Phase 1. Design initial dashboards and reports within **CJA** for key stakeholders.
  • 2.5 CDP Profile & Segment Design: Define the **unified patient profile within Real-time CDP**, including attributes, calculated insights, and consent management. Design dynamic audience segments based on patient behavior, demographics, and health needs for targeted activation via **CDP**.
  • 2.6 Integration & Activation Strategy: Outline detailed integration plans with Inova's existing marketing automation, CRM, EMR, website, and mobile platforms for seamless data flow into **AEP's Data Lake and Real-time CDP**. Plan for activation destinations (e.g., email, SMS, display ads, call center systems, personalized website content) directly from **CDP segments**.

Deliverables:

  • AEP Solution Architecture Document (with **CJA/CDP focus**)
  • Detailed XDM Data Model Schemas (optimized for **CJA/CDP**)
  • Identity Resolution Strategy & Rules Document (for **CDP unified profiles**)
  • **CJA Workspace & Data View Specifications**
  • **CDP Unified Profile & Segment Definitions**
  • Integration & Activation Plan (for **CDP activation**)

Phase 4: Pilot Testing & Optimization (Week 10)

Objective: Rigorously test the AEP implementation for data accuracy, system performance, and functional alignment with business requirements, with a strong focus on **CJA and CDP outputs**.

Key Activities:

  • 4.1 Data Quality Assurance & Reconciliation: Conduct extensive data quality checks, validating data ingestion, transformation, and identity resolution for accuracy, completeness, and consistency within **CDP profiles and CJA datasets**. Reconcile data against source systems.
  • 4.2 User Acceptance Testing (UAT): Engage business users (marketing, analytics, patient experience) in UAT sessions to validate **CJA reports, CDP segments**, and activated experiences against defined use cases. Gather feedback and iterate on configurations for **CJA and CDP**.
  • 4.3 Performance & Scalability Testing: Assess AEP system performance under expected and peak load conditions, particularly for **real-time CDP profile updates, segment activation, and CJA query performance**.
  • 4.4 Security & Compliance Audit: Conduct a thorough audit to verify that the AEP implementation adheres to all Inova's security policies and healthcare regulatory requirements (HIPAA), especially concerning **patient data within CDP**.
  • 4.5 Initial Optimization: Based on testing feedback, optimize data flows, XDM schemas, **CJA configurations, and CDP segments** for improved performance and usability.

Deliverables:

  • Comprehensive Data Quality Report (for **CDP and CJA data**)
  • UAT Sign-off & Feedback Log (for **CJA reports and CDP segments**)
  • Performance Test Results & Optimization Recommendations
  • Security & Compliance Audit Report
  • Refined AEP Configuration (including **CJA/CDP settings**)

Create detailed user guides and operational documentation for **CJA workspaces and CDP activations**.
